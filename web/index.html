Glendale, AZ

Welcome

This site is designed to distribute information about Troop 262 and its' scheduled activities.   Inside you'll find resources such as activity calendars, meeting information, maps to events, and links to other scouting resources.   Troop 262 meets weekly on Monday evening in the social center at St. Helen's Catholic Church from 7:30 to 9:00 P.M.. St. Helen's is located at the N.W. corner of 55th Avenue and Cholla Street in Glendale, Arizona.

Browse through all of our links to the left of the page to easily navigate your way through our site. Please report any broken links.   Content on our site is regularly updated. Please stop by again.   Thank you for your interest!
